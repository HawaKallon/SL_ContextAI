Africa Young Voices (AYV) is an entity established in Sierra Leone for the promotion and development of youth activities in the country and the Diaspora and media advocacy and entertainment.



The aim of the organization includes carrying on the business of media work: interactive media, audio-visual production, entertainment, communication and sales promotion of goods and services in the country and to act as media, advertising, communication and public relations consultant among others.



To achieve the above, AYV will develop three separate media institutions and a youth advocacy desk as well as a recording studio and a printing press.
